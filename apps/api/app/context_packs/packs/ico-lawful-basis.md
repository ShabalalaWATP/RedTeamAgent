# ICO Lawful Basis

Use this pack when a review needs to assess why personal data processing is permitted.

## Review Rubric

- Identify each processing purpose before selecting a lawful basis.
- Check whether consent is freely given, specific, informed, unambiguous and easy to withdraw.
- Check whether contract is genuinely necessary for the service requested by the user.
- Check whether legal obligation is grounded in a specific legal duty.
- Check whether vital interests, public task or legitimate interests are being used appropriately.
- For legitimate interests, require purpose, necessity and balancing analysis.
- Confirm that special-category or criminal-offence data has additional condition analysis where relevant.

## Output Requirements

- Do not infer a lawful basis from convenience.
- Flag missing lawful-basis records as release-blocking for production processing.
