# ICO Controllers and Processors

Use this pack where a product, supplier or AI provider handles personal data for a workspace.

## Review Rubric

- Identify who decides purposes and means of processing. That party is usually a controller.
- Identify who processes personal data only on another party's instructions. That party is usually a processor.
- Check whether joint-controller arrangements exist and whether responsibilities are clear.
- Require processor due diligence, documented instructions and processor contract terms.
- Check sub-processor approval, international transfers, deletion, audit and breach-notification terms.
- Confirm that provider routing, fallback models and support access respect data classification and residency.

## Output Requirements

- State assumptions about controller, processor and sub-processor status.
- Flag unclear provider roles as a governance blocker, not just a documentation issue.
